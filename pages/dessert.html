Mango Mango

This desert place located in Chinatown gets its name from the mango themed desserts it serves. It is a franchise and sells other items like ice-cream, crepes, and drinks. This a great hangout place with friends. They have different ice-cream flavors such as coconut, mango, matcha, and others.

Location: 1013 Cherry St, Philadelphia, PA 19107

Jeni's Splendid Ice Cream

Jeni's Ice Cream provides delicious cool treat. Although the brand started in Ohio, the popular ice cream treats spread to two locations in Philadelphia. Some popular flavors include Honey Vanilla Bean, Milkiest Chocolate, and Sweet Cream Biscuits & Peach Jam.

Location: 1901 Chestnut St, Philadelphia, PA 19103

John's Water Ice

Opened in 1945, this family buisness has specialized in making frozen treats for almost 80 years. From ice cream to gelato, they also have water-ice. From a large range of flavors, they're a great place to get a summer treat. They open from April to October.

Location: 701 Christian St, Philadelphia, PA 19147
