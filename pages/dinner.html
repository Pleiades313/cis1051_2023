El Vez

This Mexican restaurant uses great theming to create a magical dining expriences. El Vez is serves upscale Mexican eats & cocktails in a colorful, flashy setting.

Pietros Pizza

This Center City restaurant serves up delicous Italian dishes and coal fried pizzas. Pietro is a great spots to go with family or friends. Try the chicken parmesan, it is to die for!

Location: 1714 Walnut St, Philadelphia, PA 19103

Pho Ha

This Vietnames restaurant located in South Philadelphia specializes in authentic Vietnames food. Ranging from delicious warm pho soups to cho tam (a rice dish), this place is perfect if you're wanting to try something new. Recommend the bun bo hue, a spicy and more flavorful version of pho soup!

Location: 610 Washington Ave Philadelphia, PA 19147

Dae Bak

This restaurant specializes in Korean food and has a variety of cuisines. Come and try the classic duk-bokki (spicy rice cake) or the chef's special of kan poong ki (sweet and spicy fried chicken). It's the perfect place to get food with your friends after a long day.

Location: 1016 Race St 2nd Floor, Philadelphia, PA 19107
